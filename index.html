Pagsulat, Prinsipyo, at Pamilya

Hopes to make a difference through the power of words.

Hi, I’m Mariel. Nice to meet you.

I am a 21-year-old Far Eastern University - Manila BA Communication graduate. I am a trained student journalist, fueled by my interest in storytelling. My ultimate goal is to make a difference in people's lives through the power of writing. I am an active advocate for good governance, basic human rights, and social media literacy.  I graduated as the class valedictorian in junior high school and with high honors in senior high school. In college, I am a dean's lister and a student leader.

I am a LGBTQIA+ advocate who identifies herself as a Cisgender Bisexual Woman. My favorite activities are writing poetry and human interest stories. My hobby consists of watching films and television shows that pique my interest. One of my mentors gave me the best advice is "to look at the negative the same way you look at the positive side of things".
